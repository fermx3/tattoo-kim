I'm Erick, a Mexican tattoo artist exploring the Caribbean coast. I pour my heart and soul into every drop of ink, always learning and growing as an artist. I give my best in every piece and strive to create a unique, original tattoo for each client.

My passion for art takes me across new frontiers, making my way through new countries and languages to reach an international audience. I can work with you in English, French, and of course Spanish.

If you let me create wonders on your skin, you won't regret it — remember that every stroke comes with good vibes and healing for your soul's expression.
